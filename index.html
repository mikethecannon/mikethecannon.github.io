The year is 20XX. The streets are lined with chaos, debris, and the smells of wheel grease and gunpowder. In an unprecedented fit of rage, Mike Cannon-Brookes has snapped and become his alter-ego, Mike "The Cannon" Brookes.

You're going to need the power of all 5 values to board the enemy boat and get your revenge

Back our Kickstarter to unlock the final boss: Scott the Cannon on Craig Davies' boat.

How to play

Move the mouse to aim, and click to fire away.

Press Enter to restart the level.

To complete each level, break the cage that the value is in by hitting it hard enough with something.
